Our Story

Authentic Old Country Cooking.

Walk through the door and you're somewhere else. Plastic tablecloths. Yesteryear memorabilia on every wall. An old fashioned claw arcade game in the corner.

That's the whole point. We're not chasing trends. We're cooking the way it used to be cooked — with big portions, real ingredients, and the same recipes folks have been ordering for years.

Friday night fish fry. Saturday steak. Sunday family dinner. Come hungry.
